ZMU ENGL 102 The Emirates Past Present Future

Course Description

This course helps military cadets learn about the United Arab Emirates through stories, videos, and discussions. Students will explore how the UAE grew from a small desert region to a modern nation, and think about what the future might look like. The focus is on writing clearly about UAE’s journey and thinking about your role in its future.

Course Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

  1. Understand UAE’s history and how it became successful
  2. Write about UAE’s development in clear, simple essays
  3. Think about the future and what it means for them as leaders
  4. Connect past, present, and future to see how they fit together

Assignments (70% of final grade)

Essay 1: UAE’s Amazing Story (350 words)

Write about how the UAE changed from the past to today. Use examples from class materials.

Due: Week 5

Essay 2: UAE Today - What’s Special? (450 words)

Describe what makes the UAE special today. Compare it to other countries.

Due: Week 9

Essay 3: My Vision for UAE’s Future (550 words)

Think about the UAE in 20 years. What will it be like? What will you contribute?

Due: Week 12

Final Reflection (250 words)

Write about what you learned about the UAE and yourself in this course.

Due: Week 14

Weekly Activities (30% of final grade)

  • Short weekly reflections (100 words each)
  • Video discussions about UAE
  • Group projects about UAE topics
  • Class participation

Schedule

Unit 1: UAE Past - How It All Started (Weeks 1-3)

Week 1: Life Before the UAE

Focus: Understanding life in the old UAE

  • Readings:
    • Simple story about life in the desert
    • Article about pearl diving and traditional life
  • Film: Documentary clips about old UAE
  • Writing: “What I Know About Old UAE” (personal knowledge)
  • Discussion: What do you know about UAE’s past?

Week 2: Sheikh Zayed’s Vision

Focus: How one leader changed everything

  • Readings:
    • Story about Sheikh Zayed’s ideas
    • Simple explanation of UAE formation
  • Film: Documentary about UAE founding
  • Writing: Compare old UAE to new UAE
  • Discussion: What was Sheikh Zayed’s biggest contribution?

Week 3: Building the Nation

Focus: How the UAE was created

  • Readings:
    • Article about the 1971 formation
    • Story about bringing emirates together
  • Film: Clips about early UAE development
  • Writing: Essay 1 planning
  • Discussion: Why was it important to unite the emirates?

Unit 2: UAE Present - Success Today (Weeks 4-6)

Week 4: From Oil to Innovation

Focus: How UAE moved beyond oil

  • Readings:
    • Simple article about UAE’s economy
    • Story about Dubai’s growth
  • Film: “Dubai: Beyond Oil” documentary
  • Writing: Essay 1 draft workshop
  • Discussion: What makes UAE’s economy different?

Week 5: UAE as a Leader

Focus: How UAE leads in the region

  • Readings:
    • Article about UAE’s international role
    • Story about UAE’s space program
  • Film: Mars Mission highlights
  • Writing: Submit Essay 1: UAE’s Amazing Story
  • Discussion: How does UAE help other countries?

Week 6: Culture and Tradition

Focus: Balancing old and new in UAE

  • Readings:
    • Article about UAE culture
    • Story about modern Emiratis
  • Film: Clips about UAE festivals and traditions
  • Writing: Personal reflection on UAE identity
  • Discussion: How does UAE keep its culture while modernizing?

Unit 3: UAE Present - Modern Success (Weeks 7-9)

Week 7: Innovation and Technology

Focus: UAE as a tech leader

  • Readings:
    • Article about UAE’s smart cities
    • Story about digital government
  • Film: “Smart Dubai” documentary
  • Writing: Essay 2 planning
  • Discussion: How does technology make life better?

Week 8: Global Connections

Focus: UAE’s place in the world

  • Readings:
    • Article about UAE’s international relationships
    • Story about Expo 2020 Dubai
  • Film: Highlights from Expo 2020
  • Writing: Essay 2 workshop
  • Discussion: Why do other countries visit UAE?

Week 9: Youth and Education

Focus: UAE’s young people and their future

  • Readings:
    • Article about UAE education
    • Story about young Emiratis
  • Film: Clips about UAE universities and innovation
  • Writing: Submit Essay 2: UAE Today - What’s Special?
  • Discussion: What opportunities does UAE give young people?

Unit 4: UAE Future - What’s Next? (Weeks 10-12)

Week 10: Vision 2071

Focus: UAE’s plan for the future

  • Readings:
    • Simple explanation of Vision 2071
    • Article about UAE’s goals
  • Film: “UAE 2071” documentary
  • Writing: Essay 3 planning
  • Discussion: What do you want UAE to be like in 50 years?

Week 11: Environment and Sustainability

Focus: UAE’s green future

  • Readings:
    • Article about UAE’s environmental goals
    • Story about renewable energy
  • Film: “Green UAE” documentary
  • Writing: Essay 3 draft workshop
  • Discussion: How can UAE help solve environmental problems?

Week 12: Leadership in the Future

Focus: Your role as future leaders

  • Readings:
    • Article about future leaders
    • Story about youth and leadership
  • Film: Clips about young leaders
  • Writing: Submit Essay 3: My Vision for UAE’s Future
  • Discussion: What kind of leader does UAE need?

Unit 5: Your Role in UAE’s Future (Weeks 13-14)

Week 13: Contributing to UAE

Focus: How you can help UAE succeed

  • Readings:
    • Article about serving your country
    • Story about military service
  • Film: “Service to Nation” documentary
  • Writing: Final reflection planning
  • Discussion: How will you contribute to UAE?

Week 14: Course Reflection

Focus: What did you learn about UAE and yourself?

  • Activities:
    • Share favorite insights about UAE
    • Discuss how course changed your perspective
    • Peer feedback on essays
  • Writing: Final Reflection Submission
  • Discussion: Your commitment to UAE’s future

Grading System

Essay Grading (Simple 4-Point Scale)

  1. Excellent - Clear ideas, good examples, shows understanding of UAE
  2. Good - Clear main points, some examples, shows understanding
  3. Needs Improvement - Some good ideas but unclear about UAE
  4. Poor - Unclear ideas, missing examples, shows little understanding

Class Participation

  • Coming prepared with readings completed
  • Contributing to discussions about UAE
  • Participating in group activities
  • Showing respect for UAE culture and values
