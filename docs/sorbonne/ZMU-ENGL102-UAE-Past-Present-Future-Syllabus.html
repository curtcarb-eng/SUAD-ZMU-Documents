ZMU ENGL102 UAE Past Present Future Syllabus

Foresight-Based Leadership Course for Military Cadets

Zayed Military University - Sorbonne University Abu Dhabi Partnership

Course Description

This writing-intensive course examines the United Arab Emirates through the lens of strategic foresight and future studies, preparing military cadets to become leaders who can navigate complexity, anticipate change, and shape the UAE’s role as an emerging global power. Students will analyze the nation’s remarkable transformation from traditional pearl diving society to modern technological leader, while developing skills in scenario planning, trend analysis, and future-oriented leadership necessary for the UAE’s continued advancement in the 21st century.

Course Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

  1. Analyze Historical Patterns: Understand how past decisions shaped UAE’s development trajectory
  2. Evaluate Current Challenges: Assess present-day strategic issues facing the UAE
  3. Apply Foresight Methods: Use scenario planning and trend analysis for strategic thinking
  4. Synthesize Future Scenarios: Develop multiple plausible futures for UAE’s continued development
  5. Demonstrate Leadership Vision: Articulate and defend leadership approaches for future challenges
  6. Connect Global to Local: Understand UAE’s role in regional and global power dynamics
  7. Integrate Technology and Strategy: Analyze how emerging technologies drive national influence

Major Assessments (65% of final grade)

Essay 1: Foundations of UAE Leadership (900 words)

Analyze how Sheikh Zayed’s leadership philosophy established the foundation for modern UAE development. Examine how traditional Arab leadership values combined with forward-thinking vision to create unprecedented national transformation.

Due: Week 4

Essay 2: Present Day Strategic Analysis (1100 words)

Evaluate the UAE’s current position as a regional and emerging global power. Analyze specific case studies of UAE leadership in technology, diplomacy, and innovation using foresight frameworks.

Due: Week 8

Essay 3: Future Scenario Planning Project (1400 words)

Develop and analyze three plausible future scenarios for the UAE in 2040. Include strategic recommendations for military leadership preparation in each scenario.

Due: Week 12

Strategic Leadership Presentation (10 minutes)

Present your future scenario analysis with strategic recommendations for UAE military leadership in the 21st century.

Due: Week 13

Final Reflection Portfolio (600 words)

Synthesize your learning about foresight leadership and your role as a future UAE military leader in shaping the nation’s future.

Due: Week 14

Weekly Writing Tasks (35% of final grade)

  • Weekly foresight analysis papers (300-400 words)
  • Trend identification and impact assessments
  • Scenario planning worksheets
  • Peer feedback on strategic recommendations
  • Leadership vision statements

14-Week Course Schedule

UNIT 1: UAE PAST - FOUNDATIONS OF VISIONARY LEADERSHIP (Weeks 1-4)

Week 1: From Tradition to Transformation

Focus: Historical foundations and early leadership vision

  • Readings:
    • UAE Studies Textbook: Chapter on pre-federation Emirates
    • “Sheikh Zayed: Visionary Leadership” (compiled readings)
    • Historical documents on UAE formation (1971)
  • Primary Sources: Founding fathers speeches and interviews
  • Writing: Diagnostic essay - “What makes a nation?”
  • Foresight Exercise: Pattern recognition in national development
  • Discussion: Traditional leadership values in modern context

Week 2: The Oil Discovery and Strategic Decision-Making

Focus: How resource discovery transformed leadership priorities

  • Readings:
    • UAE Studies Textbook: Economic transformation chapters
    • Strategic planning documents from early oil era
    • “UAE Economic Diversification Strategy” historical analysis
  • Case Study: Abu Dhabi’s decision to invest oil revenues strategically
  • Writing: Analysis of resource-based development leadership decisions
  • Foresight Exercise: Win-lose scenario analysis (1970s oil boom)
  • Discussion: Natural resources as curse vs. blessing

Week 3: Federation as Strategic Vision

Focus: Leadership in creating unity from diversity

  • Readings:
    • UAE Studies Textbook: Federation formation process
    • Leadership documents from founding period
    • Constitutional development materials
  • Primary Sources: Founding agreements and leadership correspondence
  • Writing: Comparative analysis of federation models globally
  • Foresight Exercise: Stakeholder scenario planning (1971 formation)
  • Discussion: How diversity becomes strength in leadership

Week 4: Sheikh Zayed’s Leadership Philosophy

Focus: Visionary leadership principles and their lasting impact

  • Readings:
    • Sheikh Zayed speeches and writings on development
    • “Vision 2071” - extending Zayed’s legacy
    • Contemporary analyses of Zayed leadership model
  • Film: Documentary clips on UAE founding era
  • Writing: Submit Essay 1: Foundations of UAE Leadership
  • Foresight Exercise: Values-based scenario planning
  • Discussion: How leadership philosophy drives national transformation

UNIT 2: UAE PRESENT - LEADERSHIP IN THE GLOBAL ARENA (Weeks 5-8)

Week 5: Economic Diversification and Innovation Leadership

Focus: From oil dependency to knowledge economy

  • Readings:
    • UAE Studies Textbook: Economic diversification chapters
    • “UAE Centennial Vision 2071” implementation documents
    • Innovation strategy reports and analysis
  • Case Studies: Masdar City, Emirates Global Aluminium, ADNOC transformation
  • Writing: Strategic analysis of economic transition leadership
  • Foresight Exercise: Technology adoption scenario planning
  • Discussion: Leading through economic transformation

Week 6: Technology and Digital Leadership

Focus: UAE as regional technology hub and innovation leader

  • Readings:
    • UAE Digital Strategy 2025 documents
    • “Smart Government” implementation reports
    • Analysis of UAE technology investments and partnerships
  • Case Studies: UAE AI Strategy 2031, blockchain initiatives, Mars mission
  • Writing: Technology leadership analysis - “How UAE leads through innovation”
  • Foresight Exercise: Emerging technology impact scenarios
  • Discussion: Technology as a tool of national influence

Week 7: Diplomatic and Soft Power Leadership

Focus: UAE’s role in regional and global affairs

  • Readings:
    • UAE Foreign Policy strategic documents
    • Analysis of UAE mediation and conflict resolution roles
    • “UAE Global Compact” and international engagement materials
  • Case Studies: Yemen mediation, Abraham Accords, Expo 2020 Dubai
  • Writing: Soft power analysis - “How UAE influences without force”
  • Foresight Exercise: Geopolitical scenario planning (Middle East)
  • Discussion: Military leadership in diplomatic contexts

Week 8: Space and Science Leadership

Focus: From regional player to space-faring nation

  • Readings:
    • UAE Space Strategy documents
    • Mars Mission leadership analysis
    • Regional space cooperation frameworks
  • Case Studies: Hope Mars Mission, astronaut program, lunar mission plans
  • Writing: Submit Essay 2: Present Day Strategic Analysis
  • Foresight Exercise: Space exploration scenario planning
  • Discussion: Leadership in high-tech, high-risk ventures

UNIT 3: UAE FUTURE - STRATEGIC FORESIGHT AND EMERGING POWER (Weeks 9-12)

Week 9: Foresight Methodologies and Strategic Planning

Focus: Tools and techniques for future-oriented leadership

  • Readings:
    • “Strategic Foresight for Leaders” (adapted military applications)
    • UAE National Foresight methodology documents
    • Scenario planning best practices for government
  • Workshop: Hands-on foresight exercises using UAE case studies
  • Writing: Personal foresight assessment - “My leadership future vision”
  • Foresight Exercise: Building scenarios using structured methodologies
  • Discussion: Military applications of foresight thinking

Week 10: Climate Leadership and Sustainable Future

Focus: UAE’s role in global climate action and energy transition

  • Readings:
    • UAE Green Growth Strategy documents
    • “Net Zero by 2050” strategic pathway
    • Climate leadership analysis and international agreements
  • Case Studies: Masdar renewable energy, carbon capture initiatives, climate diplomacy
  • Writing: Sustainable leadership analysis - “Leading through environmental challenge”
  • Foresight Exercise: Climate change scenario planning for UAE
  • Discussion: Military leadership in environmental security

Week 11: Regional Security and Defense Innovation

Focus: Military leadership in an evolving security environment

  • Readings:
    • UAE Defense Strategy documents
    • Military innovation and technology integration reports
    • Regional security analysis and threat assessments
  • Case Studies: Military technology partnerships, defense industry development
  • Writing: Future military leadership requirements analysis
  • Foresight Exercise: Security scenario planning for 2035-2050
  • Discussion: Adapting military leadership for future challenges

Week 12: Global Power Projection and Influence

Focus: UAE’s trajectory toward recognized global power status

  • Readings:
    • Global power analysis reports featuring UAE
    • International relations theory applied to UAE case
    • Economic and military capability assessments
  • Writing: Submit Essay 3: Future Scenario Planning Project
  • Foresight Exercise: Final comprehensive scenario development
  • Discussion: Leadership preparation for global responsibility

UNIT 4: SYNTHESIS AND PERSONAL LEADERSHIP VISION (Weeks 13-14)

Week 13: Cadet Leadership Applications

Focus: Personal development as future UAE military leaders

  • Activities:
    • Strategic Leadership Presentations (10 minutes each)
    • Peer feedback and strategic recommendation development
    • Leadership vision refinement workshops
  • Writing: Presentation preparation and delivery
  • Foresight Exercise: Personal leadership scenario planning
  • Discussion: From individual development to national contribution

Week 14: Your Role in UAE’s Future

Focus: Synthesis and commitment to future leadership

  • Activities:
    • Final portfolio workshops and peer review
    • Leadership philosophy statement development
    • Course synthesis and reflection
  • Writing: Final Portfolio Submission with personal leadership vision
  • Foresight Exercise: 20-year personal development plan
  • Discussion: “My contribution to UAE’s Century”

Assessment Rubric Standards

Foresight-Based Essay Criteria

  1. Historical Context and Pattern Recognition (25%): Demonstrates understanding of historical causation and pattern identification
  2. Current Analysis and Strategic Assessment (25%): Accurate evaluation of present circumstances and strategic position
  3. Future Scenario Development (25%): Creates plausible, well-reasoned future scenarios using foresight methodologies
  4. Leadership Application (15%): Connects analysis to practical leadership challenges and opportunities
  5. Writing Quality and Communication (10%): Clear, persuasive academic writing with proper citation

Scenario Planning Project Standards

  • Methodological Rigor (30%): Proper use of foresight tools and techniques
  • Scenario Logic and Coherence (25%): Internal consistency and plausibility of scenarios
  • Strategic Insight (25%): Depth of analysis and strategic implications
  • Leadership Recommendations (20%): Practical, actionable advice for military leadership

Foresight Methodologies Taught

Core Foresight Tools

  1. Scenario Planning: Structured multiple futures development
  2. Trend Analysis: Identifying driving forces and weak signals
  3. STEEP Analysis: Social, Technological, Economic, Environmental, Political factors
  4. Cross-Impact Analysis: Understanding interconnections between trends
  5. Backcasting: Working backward from preferred futures to present actions

Leadership Applications

  • Strategic Vision Development: Using foresight to create compelling leadership vision
  • Risk and Opportunity Assessment: Identifying and preparing for future challenges
  • Stakeholder Scenario Planning: Understanding multiple perspectives on future outcomes
  • Adaptive Leadership: Preparing for multiple plausible futures

Course Policies and Military Context

Academic Integrity and Research Standards

  • All sources must be properly cited using academic standards
  • Plagiarism or improper attribution will result in course failure
  • Primary sources from UAE government and official sources are required for major assignments
  • Arabic language sources should be incorporated when available

Military Cadet Attendance and Participation

  • Regular attendance reflects military discipline standards
  • Active participation in foresight exercises is mandatory
  • Leadership of class discussions will be rotated among cadets
  • Peer review and feedback are considered military teamwork practice

Late Work and Revision Policy

  • Foresight exercises and scenario planning assignments cannot be submitted late due to interactive nature
  • Major essays may be revised within one week of receiving feedback
  • Revision must demonstrate substantial improvement in foresight application

Confidentiality and Security Considerations

  • Some strategic analysis may touch on sensitive national security topics
  • Class discussions should maintain appropriate confidentiality levels
  • Individual research projects will be screened for appropriate security classification

Additional Learning Outcomes for UAE Military Context

Strategic Thinking Competencies

  • Ability to think beyond immediate tactical considerations
  • Understanding of long-term strategic implications of decisions
  • Capacity to anticipate and prepare for multiple future scenarios
  • Skills in communicating strategic vision to diverse audiences

Leadership Preparation

  • Development of personal leadership philosophy aligned with UAE values
  • Understanding of leadership in diverse cultural and international contexts
  • Preparation for leadership roles in an increasingly complex global environment
  • Integration of traditional UAE values with modern strategic thinking

National Service Application

  • Direct application of foresight skills to UAE national defense planning
  • Understanding of UAE’s role in regional and global security architecture
  • Preparation for leadership roles in international military cooperation
  • Development of skills necessary for UAE’s evolving defense requirements

This syllabus represents a unique integration of UAE studies, strategic foresight, and military leadership development, preparing cadets to become visionary leaders capable of guiding the UAE through the complexities of the 21st century and beyond. The course emphasizes the UAE’s transformation from a traditional federation to a forward-looking global power, with cadets developing the foresight skills necessary to continue this trajectory.

Connection to UAE Vision 2071

This course directly supports the UAE’s Centennial Vision 2071 by:

  • Developing future-oriented leadership capabilities in military officers
  • Building strategic thinking skills necessary for national development
  • Preparing leaders who can navigate complexity and uncertainty
  • Creating officers committed to continued innovation and excellence
  • Fostering understanding of UAE’s role in the global community

The course prepares military cadets to be not just defenders of the nation, but active participants in shaping its future trajectory as a global leader in innovation, diplomacy, and strategic foresight.
